Located at the main center of Rockford Hills, Douce Alsace House owes its name to the Alsatian region in France.

Known in the whole world for these dwellings very characteristic of the Franco-German style, houses built from half-timbering.

Built-in 2017 and recently opened this summer 2019, signed a collaboration between two designers Ando Maio, a famous architect in Downtown and the designer Jessie Morena, specialised in art and numeric design. The duo had the same attachment for this particular region, in the way they decided to create a private house Alsatian typically, by the way, the materials used are totally traditional.

Insert in the heart of Rockford Hills and the neighborhood area. The advantages of this property, already by its architectural wealth, and its festive setting but calm et relaxing, moreover the property is less than 10 minutes' walk from Los Santos Golf Club and less than 20 minutes' with a vehicle from Vespucci Beach.

If you are looking for a good convalescent home, and a house full of character, this house is for you!
